# Privacy Policy for ALP (Anesthesia Learning Platform)

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

## Introduction

Welcome to ALP ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using ALP, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you register for an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Full name (display name)
- Profile picture (optional)
- Password (encrypted and stored securely)

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- Device information (device model, operating system version)
- Quiz performance and progress data
- Study statistics and learning patterns
- App usage analytics
- Error logs and crash reports
- Notification preferences

### Firebase Services
We use Firebase (a Google service) which may collect:
- Device identifiers
- IP addresses
- Usage statistics
- Performance data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
1. **Provide and Maintain Service**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Track your learning progress
   - Display personalized quiz recommendations
   - Calculate and display leaderboard rankings

2. **Improve User Experience**
   - Analyze app usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Develop new features
   - Personalize content delivery

3. **Communication**
   - Send push notifications about your progress
   - Provide customer support
   - Send important service updates
   - Notify about subscription renewals (if applicable)

4. **Security and Fraud Prevention**
   - Monitor for suspicious activity
   - Protect against unauthorized access
   - Maintain platform integrity

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
Your data is stored using:
- **Firebase Authentication**: For user account management
- **Cloud Firestore**: For app data and user progress
- **Firebase Cloud Storage**: For profile pictures and media

All data is stored on secure Google Cloud servers with industry-standard encryption.

### Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures:
- Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication mechanisms
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Encrypted password storage

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

- Share your data for marketing purposes
- Provide your email to advertisers

### We MAY share data with:
1. **Service Providers**
   - Google Firebase (hosting and authentication)

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Update
You can:
- Access your personal information in the app
- Update your profile information
- Change your password
- Modify notification preferences

### Data Deletion
You have the right to:
- Delete your account at any time
- Request deletion of all your personal data
- Export your data (upon request)

To delete your account:
1. Go to Profile > Settings
2. Select "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Opt-Out Options
You can opt-out of:
- Push notifications (in app or device settings)
- Leaderboard participation (in settings)
- Analytics (limited - some analytics are required for app functionality)
